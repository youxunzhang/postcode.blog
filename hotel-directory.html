Global flagship hotel directory

Plan business or leisure stays in the world’s headline cities with curated five-star and design-forward hotels. Browse our shortlists by region to find iconic addresses, emerging lifestyle brands, and trusted luxury chains with instant recognition.

North America

From Midtown Manhattan to the Las Vegas Strip, these hotels provide statement-making stays with award-winning service, Michelin-level dining, and fast access to major conference districts.

New York City, USA

A mix of contemporary skyscraper suites and heritage landmarks in Midtown and Downtown.

  • The St. Regis New York Fifth Avenue classic with butler service and a Beaux-Arts lobby lounge.
  • Four Seasons Hotel New York Downtown Sleek Tribeca retreat near Wall Street and the World Trade Center.
  • The Langham, New York, Fifth Avenue Spacious suites with floor-to-ceiling views near Bryant Park.

Los Angeles, USA

Resort-style luxury with rooftop pools, wellness suites, and access to studio backlots.

  • Waldorf Astoria Beverly Hills Art Deco-inspired sanctuary with panoramic rooftop dining.
  • The Beverly Hills Hotel Iconic “Pink Palace” with private bungalows and celebrity history.
  • The West Hollywood EDITION Design-forward stay steps from the Sunset Strip nightlife.

Toronto, Canada

Business-friendly hotels near Bay Street and entertainment venues around King Street West.

  • Fairmont Royal York Heritage rail hotel with modernized suites and easy Union Station access.
  • Shangri-La Toronto Asian-inspired sanctuary with acclaimed spa and opera district views.
  • Ritz-Carlton Toronto Club-level experiences overlooking Lake Ontario.

Chicago, USA

Lakefront luxury with riverfront skyline vistas and celebrated culinary programs.

  • Peninsula Chicago Asian hospitality with rooftop terrace and award-winning afternoon tea.
  • Four Seasons Hotel Chicago Magnificent Mile address with art deco styling.
  • Langham Chicago Mies van der Rohe landmark featuring Chuan Spa and Club lounge.

Europe

Historic palaces, boutique townhouse retreats, and avant-garde design hotels within walking distance of cultural icons, Michelin-starred dining, and luxury shopping corridors.

London, United Kingdom

Central London stays near Mayfair, Knightsbridge, and the City of London.

  • The Savoy Thames-side heritage hotel with private butlers and storied theater connections.
  • Claridge’s Art Deco masterpiece known for refined afternoon tea and bespoke suites.
  • The Connaught Michelin-starred dining and Aman Spa in the heart of Mayfair.

Paris, France

Haussmann-era grandeur steps from the Champs-Élysées, Avenue Montaigne, and the Louvre.

  • Hôtel Plaza Athénée Couture address with Dior Spa and Eiffel Tower panoramas.
  • Le Bristol Paris Palace hotel featuring a three-Michelin-starred restaurant and leafy courtyard.
  • The Peninsula Paris Rooftop dining and state-of-the-art suites near Arc de Triomphe.

Rome, Italy

Boutique palazzi blending Roman heritage with contemporary luxury amenities.

  • Hotel de Russie Villa Borghese hideaway with terraced gardens and city skyline views.
  • JK Place Roma Tailored townhouse experience minutes from the Spanish Steps.
  • Bulgari Hotel Roma Marble-clad suites and signature spa on Piazza Augusto Imperatore.

Berlin, Germany

Modern luxury near the Brandenburg Gate with bold architecture and creative dining.

  • Hotel Adlon Kempinski Iconic address overlooking Pariser Platz and the Gate.
  • The Ritz-Carlton Berlin Potsdamer Platz hotel featuring Fragrances cocktail bar.
  • Soho House Berlin Members-club style rooms with rooftop pool in Mitte.

Asia-Pacific

Skyline suites, urban resorts, and waterfront getaways in Asia-Pacific hubs known for innovation, finance, and next-level hospitality.

Tokyo, Japan

Skyscraper sanctuaries with cutting-edge technology and serene wellness programs.

  • Aman Tokyo Minimalist urban retreat with ryokan-inspired design and skyline pool.
  • Park Hyatt Tokyo Shinjuku tower made famous by cinematic city views.
  • The Peninsula Tokyo Imperial Palace panoramas and Rolls-Royce house cars.

Hong Kong SAR, China

Victoria Harbour spectacles with world-class dining and club lounges.

  • The Upper House Contemporary calm above Pacific Place with Café Gray Deluxe.
  • Rosewood Hong Kong Art-filled harborfront icon with Asaya wellness concept.
  • Four Seasons Hotel Hong Kong Multi-Michelin-starred dining and harbor-edge infinity pools.

Sydney, Australia

Harbour-view hideaways balancing cosmopolitan energy with coastal relaxation.

  • Park Hyatt Sydney Opera House views from harborside balconies in The Rocks.
  • Crown Towers Sydney Resort-style amenities within Barangaroo’s waterfront precinct.
  • QT Sydney Art Deco boutique hotel with playful interiors near Pitt Street Mall.

Singapore

Strategic business hub with integrated resorts and lush city gardens.

  • Marina Bay Sands Signature infinity pool and observation deck above Marina Bay.
  • Raffles Singapore Colonial-style suites with personal butler service and famed Long Bar.
  • Capella Singapore Sentosa Island resort with villas set amid rainforest grounds.

Middle East & Africa

Desert city skylines, Red Sea resorts, and urban art scenes supported by premium hospitality brands.

Dubai, United Arab Emirates

Statement towers and beachfront retreats with Michelin-starred experiences.

  • Burj Al Arab Jumeirah Sail-shaped icon with duplex suites and private beach access.
  • Atlantis The Royal Ultra-luxury resort on Palm Jumeirah with celebrity chef dining.
  • Address Sky View Twin-tower downtown hotel with rooftop infinity pool facing Burj Khalifa.

Doha, Qatar

Modern waterfront developments blending Arab heritage and global events infrastructure.

  • Mandarin Oriental Doha Souq-inspired retreat with desert-hued interiors in Msheireb Downtown.
  • W Doha Vibrant design hotel with award-winning restaurants on West Bay.
  • Ritz-Carlton Doha Private island resort featuring marina access and luxury villas.

Cape Town, South Africa

Table Mountain backdrops paired with coastal vineyards and design-led stays.

  • One&Only Cape Town Waterfront resort with private island spa and panoramas.
  • Silo Hotel Contemporary art hotel rising above the Zeitz MOCAA museum.
  • Ellerman House Boutique clifftop mansion with private art collection in Bantry Bay.

Johannesburg, South Africa

Business-centric hotels in Sandton and Rosebank with elevated security and service.

  • Saxon Hotel, Villas & Spa Secluded estate with award-winning spa in Sandhurst.
  • Four Seasons Hotel The Westcliff Hillside retreat overlooking the Johannesburg Zoo.
  • The Leonardo Premium residences and hotel floors within Africa’s tallest tower.

Latin America

Vibrant cultural capitals delivering boutique sophistication, rooftop nightlife, and coastal escapes.

Mexico City, Mexico

Polanco and Reforma properties blending historic mansions with modern high-rises.

  • Four Seasons Hotel Mexico City Garden courtyard sanctuary on Paseo de la Reforma.
  • Las Alcobas, a Luxury Collection Hotel Intimate Polanco boutique with handwoven details.
  • St. Regis Mexico City Skyline views and Remède Spa services in Reforma corridor.

São Paulo, Brazil

Financial hub hotels with high-rise art collections and rooftop pools.

  • Hotel Unique Iconic architecture with Skye rooftop bar overlooking Ibirapuera Park.
  • Palácio Tangará Urban resort surrounded by Burle Marx Park with Michelin-starred dining.
  • Rosewood São Paulo Vertical garden towers within Cidade Matarazzo complex.

Buenos Aires, Argentina

Belle Époque mansions and contemporary towers near Recoleta and Puerto Madero.

  • Alvear Palace Hotel Classic luxury with Louis XV décor and rooftop views.
  • Faena Hotel Buenos Aires Philippe Starck design with tango performances in Puerto Madero.
  • Palacio Duhau – Park Hyatt Buenos Aires Mansion and modern wing connected by landscaped gardens.

Lima, Peru

Clifftop Miraflores escapes with award-winning culinary programs.

  • Belmond Miraflores Park Oceanfront suites with rooftop pool and Observatory Lounge breakfast.
  • JW Marriott Hotel Lima Glass-clad tower with panoramic Pacific views.
  • Hotel B Artsy Barranco boutique with curated Peruvian art collection.
